Welcome

Hello and welcome to the exciting world of electronics!

Throughout the Creative Electronics Guide you will learn:
  • The basics of making a circuit
  • How to program electronics using Arduino
  • How to use components as inputs and outputs
  • and a whole lot more...

Things you need

To complete all of the projects in this guide you will need the following items:
  • Arduino/Genuino 101 board
  • USB Cable - Standard A-B.
  • White LEDs - They come in 3mm, 5mm, 10mm sizes. If you can't get white, any color will do.
  • 220 ohm resistors
  • 10K ohm resistors
  • Photo cell - We will use this as our light sensor.
  • Servo motor
  • Jumper wires
  • Breadboard
  • A computer with internet connection
  • Various arts and crafts supplies
  • Fiberfill (poly stuffing) or cotton balls
