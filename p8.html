Self-designed Project

Now it's your time to get creative and design your own project!

Your goal:
  • Incorporate all of the skills that we praticed in the guide (LEDs, light sensor, servo motor).
  • Create instructions for someone else to try out your projects
  • Make sure your project incorporates arts and crafts materials. Recycled materials like cups, boxes, and paper tubes make excellent Arduino parts!
  • Create a short video demonstrating your creation.
  • Take a moment to reflect on all of the awesome skills you have learned and about the many ways you can use them going forward!

More Resources

If your curious about why we use resistors and how to choose the correct values, Sparkfun has a great tutorial on Voltage, Current, Resistance, and Ohm's Law

If you are interested in learning to program the Arduino using written code, there are some great guides on the Arduino website

If you want to know more about Scratch programming, the Scratch Video Tutorials are a great place to look

Have fun and stay creative!
